The Basics of CUDA Programming

GPU architecture

As of writing this article in 2025, the GPU market is dominated by Nvidia GPUs. These GPUs offer an enormous computing power compared to conventional CPUs. However, programming these devices to exploit their computing capabilities requires writing programs which take into account the structure and layout of the device. This article explores that and the associated concepts.
